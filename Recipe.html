Recipe

rdfs:Class Type
A recipe. This document contains a list of terms that have been enumerated explicitly. This vocabularies define the concepts and relationships (also referred to as “terms”) used to describe and represent a recipe.
Property Expected Type Description
Object Properties
hasTotalTime TotalTime The total time it takes to follow all the steps. It consists of the cookTime, chillTime & prepTime.
hasMealType MealType The type of the meal, it can either be Breakfast, Brunch, Lunch, Dinner, or Snack.
hasRecipeType RecipeType The type of recipe, it can be Appetizer, Dessert, Salads, e.t.c.
hasRecipeCuisine RecipeCuisine The classification of Recipe, as per Cuisine type. It can be Asian, French, Italian e.t.c.
hasDifficultyLevel DifficultyLevel The difficulty level of the recipe, It can be easy, medium or hard.
wasDerivedFrom Source The source, URL where the recipe the recipe was originally published.
produceQuantityOfFood QuantityOfFood The quantity produced by the recipe (for example, number of people served, number of servings, etc).
tool CookingEquipment An object used (but not consumed) when performing instructions or a direction.
cookingMethod CookingMethod The method of cooking, such as Frying, Steaming, e.t.c.
recipeYield FoodProduct The food product yield after the completion of recipe i.e. Cupcakes.
recipeIngredient Ingredient An Ingredient used in the recipe.
hasMainIngredient MainIngredient  or
Ingredient 		The Main ingredient used in the recipe i.e. Chicken.
hasSideIngredient SideIngredient  or
Ingredient 		The side ingredient used in the recipe i.e. Potato.
hasRecipeInstruction Instruction A step in making the recipe, in the form of a single item (document, video, etc.)
Object Properties from schema.org
aggregateRating AggregateRating The overall rating, based on a collection of reviews or ratings, of the item.
author Organization  or
Person 		The author of this content or rating. Please note that author is special in that HTML 5 provides a special mechanism for indicating authorship via the rel tag. That is equivalent to this and may be used interchangeably.
Data Properties
hasTitle Text The Title of the recipe.
Data Properties from schema.org
image ImageObject  or
URL 		An image of the item. This can be a URL or a fully described ImageObject.

Hammad's World Famous Banana Bread
By John Smith, May 8, 2009
<img src="bananabread.jpg" alt="Banana bread on a plate" />

This classic banana bread recipe comes from my mom -- the walnuts add a nice
 texture and flavor to the banana bread.

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 1 hour
Yield: 1 loaf
Tags: Low fat

Nutrition facts:
240 calories, 9 grams fat

Ingredients:
- 3 or 4 ripe bananas, smashed
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup of sugar
...

Instructions:
 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Mix in the ingredients in a bowl. Add the
 flour last. Pour the mixture into a loaf pan and bake for one hour.
